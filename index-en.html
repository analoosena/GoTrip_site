Arraial do Cabo brings together some of the most beautiful features of the Brazilian coast, with lagoons and beaches with crystal-clear seas, white sand dunes, and restinga vegetation, as well as cliffs that act as viewpoints to appreciate it all. Known as the “Capital of Diving, Paradise of the Atlantic and the Caribbean of Rio de Janeiro”, come and discover the history of this place.

Itacaré - BA

Itacaré belongs to the so-called “Cocoa Coast”, bordering the municipalities of Maraú, Aurelino Leal, Ubaitaba, Uruçuca, Ilhéus and Oceano Atlântico. It is within the tropical climate range, with an average temperature of 27ºC. It has an area of ​​approximately 732km² and its most important geographic feature is the Rio de Contas.
